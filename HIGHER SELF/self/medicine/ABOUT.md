# Medicine

Medicine is a collection of truths that shift your state when you read them at the right moment.

Not affirmations. Not motivational quotes. These are things you've realized through experience — concentrated truths that cut through noise when you're stuck, triggered, or caught in a loop.

Think of it as a personal pharmacy. When a pattern is active or you can't see clearly, the right truth at the right moment works like medicine.

## What belongs here

A statement belongs in this collection if:

- It can shift your state when you read it at the right moment
- It's concentrated — a truth, not an explanation
- It can stand alone without context

## Examples

> The meaning I assign to this situation is the experience I get.

> I don't need to see the whole path. I just need to see the next step.

> This feeling is not a fact.

> What I resist persists. What I look at dissolves.

> The physical mind was never designed to figure out the "how."

## How it grows

You don't sit down and write medicine. It shows up — in conversations, in books, in moments of clarity. The AI extracts it automatically when processing intake, and you can flag it yourself: "That's medicine."

## How it works

The AI isn't just collecting — it's prescribing. When a pattern is active and you're caught in a loop, the AI pulls the right truth from your medicine collection as an antidote. Each pattern file can have its own set of prescriptions drawn from here.

Over time, this collection grows into something deeply personal and useful.
