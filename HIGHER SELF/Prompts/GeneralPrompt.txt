Some things you can say to your Higher Self to get started:

--- First conversation ---
"Hi. I want you to read the Higher Self skill and then let's set up my state. Here's who I am: [tell it about yourself]"

--- Daily check-in ---
"How are we doing? Here's where I'm at today: [share what's on your mind]"

--- Process intake ---
"I just watched/read something interesting. Process this for me: [paste transcript or drop file into RAW Input/Intake/]"

--- Get guidance ---
"What should I focus on right now?"
"I'm stuck. Help me see what's going on."
"I keep doing [X pattern] — can you help me understand why?"

--- Pattern work ---
"I noticed I keep [describe behavior]. Is this a pattern?"
"[Pattern name] is active right now. What do I need to see?"

--- End of session ---
"Please update the system and all relevant files according to our conversation"

--- Life logistics ---
"Add [task] to my floating tasks"
"What's on my plate right now?"

--- Going deeper ---
"What beliefs might be running underneath this?"
"Help me investigate: is [belief] actually true?"
"What meaning am I assigning to this situation?"
